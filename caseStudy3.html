Implementing a tracking framework for our products and helping implement segment and Mixpanel


Reading time: 4 mins

Project Overview

At LJ Hooker, the products we build, are used by LJHooker offices all across the country as well as their clients. However, there was not much emphasis put on defining and measuring the success or failure of features and products.

Through this project I successfully implemented Segment and Mixpanel. I was able to define, implement events, and measure what success for our client facing and agent facing products were.

My role

I decided to change this out of shear frustration. I wanted to start becoming product-led instead of feature-led.

I created a metrics framework that works for our business, defined the success metrics for both our consumer facing and internal/Agent facing tools.

After which, with the help of our FE engineers, we determined a tracking plan to implement all the events and properties we want to capture.

The problem

We were building products without defining success metrics and therefore not measuring if we were on the right track. This often came up as a blocker as no one could accurately measure how much of usage our products were getting apart from basic google analytics.
